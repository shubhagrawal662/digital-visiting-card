# Startup Roadmap

## Phase 1 (MVP+)
- OTP login, catalog, cart, checkout, order tracking
- Admin product/order management
- Rider assignment + live location

## Phase 2 (Growth)
- Referral, wallet, loyalty, multilingual expansion
- AI recommendations and demand forecasting
- Subscription (daily milk/vegetables)

## Phase 3 (Scale)
- Multi-city microservice split
- Inventory automation and surge pricing
- Warehouse and farmer payout automation
